Categories

Maths tests in each of the three categories (GCSE, A-Level, University) cover the most, if not all, the topics required within the relevant level. All tests were designed to enable students to revise and learn more Maths before an exam. The categories are divided into subcategories to ensure comfortable search of the relevant test.

GCSE MATHEMATICS

Section for The General Certificate of Secondary Education students. GCSE level includes mathematical tests covering requirements for both Foundation and Higher GCSE. Maths tests in this category will help you prepare in fields such as algebra, arithmetics, factors, linear equations, matrices and more. Here is the list with available sections with tests:
